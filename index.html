Help & FAQ

How to Play

1. Click on cells to reveal what's underneath

2. Find all diamonds to complete the level

3. Avoid mines - they will end your game

4. Complete levels to earn USD rewards

5. Use coins to skip levels or restart when needed

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn money?
You earn money by completing levels. Each completed level gives you $0.005 USD. You can also earn through the referral program and daily bonuses.
How do I withdraw my earnings?
Go to the Withdraw section, enter your details, and request a withdrawal. The minimum withdrawal amount is $5.00. Withdrawals are processed within 24-48 hours.
What is the referral program?
Share your referral link with friends. When they join using your link and verify their account, contact admin to receive a reward code that you can redeem for coins or USD.
Why do I need to join the Telegram channel?
Joining our Telegram channel helps us build a community and keeps you updated with the latest news, updates, and special promotions related to Minezwall.
What happens if I hit a mine?
If you hit a mine, the game ends. You can either watch an ad to continue, use coins to skip the level, or restart the level (which costs coins).
